Mini Cheese Bread

Description

I thought this up one night when I was craving cheese bread. I lived alone and I can't stand wasting food, so I needed a quick solution. I found that the key to really good cheese bread is full fat cheese and a lot of butter.

Ingredients

  • 6 in. pita rounds
  • butter
  • minced garlic
  • full fat mozzarella
  • feta crumbles
  • italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Melt the butter and mix in a tbsp of garlic
  2. Brush pita rounds with a generous amount of butter mixture
  3. Sprinkle on shreds of mozzarella and a few crumbles of feta
  4. Bake at 400 until cheese is melted, then top broil until the edges are crisp
