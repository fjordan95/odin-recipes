Chili Recipe

Description

My mom used to make us chili all the time when we were kids. Then when I got older, more of the cooking fell to me. I worked up this chili recipe on my own based on all the times I had seen my mom make chili when I was younger.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. of ground beef
  • one can of tomato sauce
  • one can of black beans
  • one can of refried beans
  • one can of kidney beans
  • one can of ranch style beans
  • chili spices to taste
  • corn chips, optional
  • cheese, optional

Steps

  1. Brown the beef in a large pot, then drain excess fat when done
  2. Add all other ingredients to pot and cook on low-medium heat for 20-25 minutes until all of the food is hot
  3. Serve with corn chips and cheese if you would like
