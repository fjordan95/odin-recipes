Spicy Spinach Artichoke Pasta

Description

I was craving pasta one day and found this fantastic recipe. It reheats well and the pepper adds and extra kick that keep the dish interesting. I don't have a sentimental story to go with this one. The pasta just tastes really good.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. of cooked and shredded chicken breast
  • one box of penne pasta
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • minced garlic
  • crushed red pepper flakes
  • 3 cups of baby spinach
  • a pint of chicken stock
  • one block of cream cheese
  • about a cup of heavy cream
  • one can of quartered artichoke hearts

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat the oil then add three cloves of garlic and red pepper flakes to taste
  2. Add the baby spinach and cook until the leaves are throroughly wilted
  3. Mix in the cream cheese and heavy cream in small amounts until cream cheese is smooth
  4. Add chicken stock and artichoke hearts then bring to a boil
  5. Lower heat, then add uncooked pasta and cover for 20 minutes stirring occasionally
  6. Add chicken and mix well then remove from heat
  7. Let pasta rest for 10 minutes then serve
